The Studio

Hello, I’m Khushi.

I draw patterns the way other people keep journals — obsessively, from life, and always with a repeat in mind.

For a decade I have helped homeware, apparel and lifestyle brands find a visual language of their own. My work sits at the seam between fine craft and clean production: motifs sketched by hand, then engineered into files a mill or press can actually run.

I studied textile design, spent years in-house at a home-decor label, and now run a small independent studio taking on a handful of considered projects each season.
